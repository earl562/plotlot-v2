The Collection

Each skill targets a different aspect of design quality. Copy one file, reference it, and the AI follows the rules.

Configuration

Three dials, infinite range

The taste-skill has three configurable settings. Adjust them based on what you're building.

DESIGN_VARIANCE
How experimental the layout is. Low values give clean, centered grids. High values give asymmetric, whitespace-heavy, modern layouts.
1-3
Standard
4-7
Overlapping
8-10
Asymmetric
MOTION_INTENSITY
How much animation there is. Low keeps it subtle. High gives you magnetic effects, spring physics, and scroll-triggered choreography.
1-3
Simple hovers
4-7
Fade-ins, smooth scroll
8-10
Spring physics
VISUAL_DENSITY
How much content fits on one screen. Low is spacious and luxurious. High is dense and compact, built for dashboards and data-heavy interfaces.
1-3
Luxury
4-7
Normal
8-10
Dashboard
Getting Started

Two steps. That's it.

1

Copy the skill file

Grab the SKILL.md from whichever skill folder you need and drop it into your project root. You can use one skill or combine several.

2

Reference it in your AI editor

Tell your AI to read and follow it. In Cursor, type @SKILL.md. In other editors, paste the contents or point to the file. The AI reads the rules and applies them to every component it generates.
